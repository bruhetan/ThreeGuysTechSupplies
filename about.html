About Us

Three Guys: Tech Supplies is a small business founded by three technology enthusiasts who were frustrated with the lack of affordable and high-quality computer parts available to consumers. Determined to provide a better shopping experience, they decided to create their own store.

Our Mission

Our mission at Three Guys: Tech Supplies is to be the one-stop-shop for all of your computer part needs. Whether you're a seasoned computer builder or a novice, we want to make the selection process simple and straightforward.

In addition to providing a comprehensive selection of components, we've also created pre-built desktop computers that are perfect for individuals who are new to the world of computers. These pre-built systems include everything you need to get started so that you can spend more time enjoying your new computer and less time worrying about compatibility issues or component selection.

Why Choose Three Guys: Tech Supplies?

At Three Guys: Tech Supplies, we're passionate about technology and we're committed to providing you with the best possible shopping experience. Here are just a few reasons why you should choose us:

  • Our prices are competitive
  • We offer a comprehensive selection of computer components and pre-built desktop systems
  • Our team is knowledgeable and always willing to provide assistance
  • We offer fast and reliable shipping services so that you can start building or using your new computer as soon as possible

Our Name

You may be curious about our name, Three Guys: Tech Supplies. We were inspired by the popular fast-food chain Five Guys: Burgers and Fries, but we wanted to put our own spin on it. Our name reflects the fact that we're a small team of passionate and knowledgeable individuals who are dedicated to providing you with the best possible technology solutions.
